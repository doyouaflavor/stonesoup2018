石頭湯計畫
Stone Soup

不同於其他動物，人在吃飯時是最放下心防的。我們喜歡分享，喜歡約朋友一起吃飯，在用餐期間暢聊談心。
石頭湯計劃便是由此觀念出發，用一碗簡單、美味、健康的料理，讓你走向平常只敢在一定距離外偷偷觀察的人們。我們在路上不敢直視街友或其他外形與自己不同的人，害怕相交的眼神會顯得侵略、使對方感到被冒犯。但這樣的迴避也突顯出許多社會普遍不敢面對的問題。

Human beings tend not to defend themselves when they are having meals, and it is the reason why we like to ask friends for eating together and chatting with each other.
Stone Soup Plan makes people get closer with others who look different from us through sharing a simple, delicious and healthy meal. This plan aims to eliminate the problem of social exclusion, which stems from the fact that people are not comfortable with looking at others, but it is this fear makes people ignore those social issues.
